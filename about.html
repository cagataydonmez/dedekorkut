Mobirise

DEDE KORKUT Guitar Pedals, is a small company located in Turkey, Europe, specialized in designing and manufacturing DSP based guitar pedals. Our main objective is to maintain high audio quality while decreasing costs, and offering reasonable, competitive and affordable prices.

We started our journey in 2014, designing hand made analog guitar pedals for local musicians and friends. Since our team consists of an electronics engineer and a musician, producer, we moved on designing our own guitar pedals, that we like to design and fun to play.

We love working hard, designing new gear and creating algorithms.
