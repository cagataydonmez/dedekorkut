FAQ

Common questions from players.

Where should these pedals sit in my signal chain?

A practical starting point is: gain pedals → Dynamic Tremolo → Ferromagnetic → Reverberant. Adjust to taste for experimental sounds.

Are they suitable for studio and live use?

Yes. The controls are designed for repeatable settings in studio sessions and quick adjustments on stage.

Which styles are they best for?

Ambient, indie, worship, post-rock, blues, soul, and cinematic guitar work all pair well with these pedals.

Can I request setup guidance before buying?

Absolutely. Contact us with your guitar, amp, and genre and we’ll suggest a practical pedal order and settings direction.
