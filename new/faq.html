FAQ

Common questions from players.

Where should these pedals sit in my signal chain?

A practical starting point is: gain pedals → Dynamic Tremolo/Crowded Chorus/Many-Phased God → Ferromagnetic → Reverberant. Adjust to taste for experimental sounds.

Are they suitable for studio and live use?

Yes. The controls are designed for repeatable settings in studio sessions and quick adjustments on stage.

Which styles are they best for?

Ambient, indie, worship, post-rock, blues, soul, cinematic guitar work, and classic modulation-heavy styles all pair well.

Where can I see older pedals?

Visit the Legacy page for archive-era models collected from social channels and historical product listings.
