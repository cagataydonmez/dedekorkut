Dynamic Tremolo

Dynamic Tremolo follows your right hand. Build subtle sway, aggressive chop, or tempo-like pulses, then blend ambience for richer width.

  • Tone character: clear transients from smooth pulse to hard rhythmic gating.
  • Playing styles: surf, soul, indie, blues, Americana, and cinematic clean tones.
  • Best placement: after gain and before delay/reverb for classic behavior.
  • Live feel: dynamic response adds expression without extra footwork.
