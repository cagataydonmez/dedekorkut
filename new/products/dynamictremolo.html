Dynamic Tremolo

Dynamic Tremolo moves with your right hand. Dial smooth pulse, edgy ramps, or rhythmic chopping—then blend in ambience for wider motion without adding complexity.

  • Tone character: clear transient definition from subtle sway to hard chop.
  • Guitar styles: surf, soul, indie, blues, Americana, cinematic clean soundscapes.
  • Pedalboard placement: after gain and before delay/reverb for classic behavior.
  • Studio use: great for rhythmic movement that keeps low-end controlled.
  • Live use: dynamic response adds feel without tap-dance complexity.
  • Signal-chain compatibility: stacks cleanly with delays and broad reverbs.
