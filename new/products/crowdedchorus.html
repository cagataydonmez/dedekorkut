Chorus / Vibrato

Crowded Chorus

Crowded Chorus delivers lush modulation from subtle widening to deep pitch movement. It can sit as a clean chorus enhancer or move into fully wet vibrato textures.

  • Tone character: warm chorus body with detailed high-end movement.
  • Mode switch: chorus blend mode and pure vibrato style movement.
  • Playing styles: dream-pop, indie, ambient clean, and retro modulation parts.
  • Best placement: after drives and before delay/reverb for dimensional modulation.
