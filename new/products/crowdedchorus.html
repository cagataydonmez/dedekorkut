Dynamic Chorus & Vibrato

Crowded Chorus

Crowded Chorus is designed for players who want width and motion without losing control over the center of their sound.

Modes

  • Single mode keeps the modulation focused and direct.
  • Multi mode opens the sound into a broader, more layered chorus texture.
  • Vibrato mode shifts the pedal into a more pitch-forward movement when you want less blend and more character.

Control Feel

  • Depth and Speed handle the motion shape directly, while Tone and Space help place the effect inside a rig more naturally.
  • Mix makes it easy to keep the pedal subtle for widening or stronger for obvious modulation.
  • Normal and Dynamic modes let the effect either stay steady or respond more actively to your touch.

Best For

  • Players who want chorus and vibrato in one compact pedal.
  • Boards where modulation needs to move from gentle width to more expressive movement.
  • Indie, dream-pop, soul, and ambient setups that benefit from warmer chorus textures.
