Multi Space Reverb

Reverberant

Reverberant keeps six useful spaces close at hand, from grounded room and spring textures to larger ambient washes.

Voice Set

  • Bank A covers Hall, Plate, and Spring for classic utility and familiar depth.
  • Bank B adds Large Hall, Room, and Shimmer when you need broader or more cinematic space.
  • The control layout stays compact, so you can move between sounds quickly without a screen or hidden menu.

Controls

  • Decay shapes tail length for short support or longer wash.
  • Pre-Delay helps the dry signal stay articulate before the reverb blooms.
  • Tone and Space let you tune the reverb around your guitar and amp instead of forcing a fixed voicing.
  • Mix ranges from supportive depth to full wet output for more experimental setups.

Best For

  • Players who need one reverb pedal to cover both practical and atmospheric roles.
  • Ambient, indie, worship, and cinematic guitar work where reverb character matters as much as size.
  • Boards that need repeatable stage settings without sacrificing studio nuance.
