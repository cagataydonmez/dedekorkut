Tape Delay

Ferromagnetic

Ferromagnetic delivers tape-style depth and rhythmic playability with fixed and multi playback head behavior. It’s designed for guitarists who need evocative repeats without losing note definition.

  • Tone character: warm repeats, dynamic high-end roll-off, musical movement.
  • Guitar styles: ambient, post-rock, blues, indie lead and rhythmic textures.
  • Pedalboard placement: usually after drives, before wide reverb.
  • Studio use: controlled repeat density that stays out of the vocal pocket.
  • Live use: quick rhythmic subdivisions for dotted/triplet performance parts.
  • Signal-chain compatibility: pairs well with overdrives, compression, and modulation.
