Tape Delay

Ferromagnetic

Ferromagnetic delivers tape-style depth and rhythmic playability with fixed and multi playback-head behavior. It is made for players who want expressive repeats without losing note definition.

  • Tone character: warm repeats, musical saturation, and dynamic top-end rolloff.
  • Playing styles: ambient, post-rock, blues, indie leads, and rhythmic delay parts.
  • Best placement: after gain, before big reverb for classic dimensional space.
  • Stage workflow: fast rhythmic selection for dotted and triplet performance parts.
