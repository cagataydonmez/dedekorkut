Multi Head Tape Delay

Ferromagnetic

Ferromagnetic is built for players who want tape-style movement, tactile control, and repeat behavior that stays musical instead of vague.

Why It Stands Out

  • Switch between Fixed Head and Multi Head behavior without leaving the core delay workflow.
  • Blend three playback heads into rhythmic repeat patterns that feel easy to set and easy to repeat live.
  • Shape both time and tone from cleaner repeats to darker, more worn tape character.
  • Push Head A up to 2000 ms for larger ambient passages and slower cinematic delay work.

Controls

  • Time / Speed for repeat timing and movement.
  • Tape Age for the bandwidth and worn-in character of the repeats.
  • Head Distance for playback spacing without losing the pedal’s basic tape feel.
  • Mix and Feedback for balancing clarity, depth, and sustain.

Use Cases

  • Ambient and post-rock players who want wide rhythmic repeats without menu diving.
  • Studio sessions where repeat texture needs to shift quickly from articulate to aged.
  • Live boards that need one delay pedal to cover utility echoes and more character-driven tape work.
