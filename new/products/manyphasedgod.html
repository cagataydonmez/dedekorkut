Many-Phased God

Many-Phased God is built for players who want more than one phaser flavor. It covers subtle movement, deep resonant sweep, and animated vintage-style phase tones.

  • Tone character: smooth sweep with controllable resonance and stage complexity.
  • Voice concept: multi-stage topology for broader phase texture options.
  • Playing styles: funk rhythm, psychedelic leads, synth-like clean textures, cinematic beds.
  • Best placement: before delay/reverb, after gain for pronounced movement.
