Phaser

Many-Phased God

Many-Phased God is built for players who want more than one phaser personality without sacrificing front-panel control.

Phase Character

  • Vintage mode leans toward narrower, more familiar sweep.
  • Classic mode opens up a broader phaser movement for traditional modulation parts.
  • Multi 8 pushes into denser, more dramatic phase texture when you want the pedal to become part of the arrangement.

Controls

  • Depth, Speed, and Feedback give direct control over sweep intensity and resonance.
  • Space helps position the movement so it can stay supportive or become more immersive.
  • Volume keeps output balance easy on a live board.
  • Normal and Dynamic modes let the phaser stay steady or react more actively to your playing approach.

Best For

  • Players who want a phaser that covers both classic movement and more animated textures.
  • Modulation-heavy rigs that still need quick, practical front-panel recall.
  • Psych, soul, ambient, and alternative setups where phase should feel expressive rather than generic.
